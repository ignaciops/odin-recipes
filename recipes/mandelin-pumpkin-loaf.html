Mandelin Pumpkin Loaf

This is a recipe shared on Gif Recipes subreddit. It is a Lush, dense, full of pumpkin flavor and perfectly spiced all favore, according to the author.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Natural Almond Flour
  • 1 cup Oat flour
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin spice
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup pumpkin purée
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil

Optional ingredients

  • 8 ounces whipped cream cheese
  • 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1/2 Natural diced almonds

Steps:

For the bread:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 defrees.
  2. Line an 8.5" x 4.5" inch loaf with parchment paper.
  3. In a medium sized bowl, whisk together dry ingredients.
  4. Add eggs, pumpkin purée and coconut oil to dry ingredients, mix well.
  5. Pour batter into lined loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees. Loaf is ready when a toothpick inserted into the center ocmes out clean.
  7. Transfer to cooling rack and remove loaf from pan. Cool 30 minutes before frosting.

For the frosting:

  1. Whisk whipped cream cheese and powdered sugar together until smooth.
  2. Top with chopped or diced almonds
