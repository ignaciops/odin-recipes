One Pan Tomato Egg Tortilla

This recipe was posted in the Gif Recipes subreddit. It is a One Pan tortilla with various delicious ingredients in one plate, great for breakfast and children's snacks. You can enjoy a different kind of fun at home with just one pan.

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion
  • 150g cheese
  • 1 tomato
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 sheets mozzarela cheese
  • 70g mozzarella cheese
  • 70g spinach
  • Salt, pepper, tomato sauce as you may want.

Steps:

  1. Cut an onion into tiny slices.
  2. Oil the pan and add sliced onions.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and pepper and stir-fry the onions until golden brown.
  4. Lay out a sheet of tortilla without oil in the pan.
  5. Put 4 eggs on top and add salt and peppero on the yolk.
  6. Add the sauteed onion, chopped spinach and cheese and cover with a tortilla.
  7. Cover with a lid and cook on low heat for 5 minutes.
  8. When one side is crispy, flip and spread tomato sauce on top.
  9. Top it off with chopped spinach, sliced tomatoes and mozzarella cheese.
  10. Cover and cook over low heat until cheese melts.
